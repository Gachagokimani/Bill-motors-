Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What documents do I need to rent a car?

To rent a car, you'll need:

  • Valid driver's license
  • Credit card in the renter's name
  • Proof of insurance
  • Valid ID or passport

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 24 hours before pickup. Cancellations within 24 hours may be subject to a fee.

Is insurance included in the rental price?

Basic insurance is included. Additional coverage options are available for purchase.
